Spot the Phishing Email

Test your skills! Hover over different parts of this suspicious email to learn what makes it dangerous.

From: security@verify-account-bank.com
Subject: URGENT: Your account will be closed in 24 hours

Dear Customer,

We detected suspicious activity on your account.

You must verify your identity immediately or your account will be permanently closed.

Click here to verify your account

Thank you for you're cooperation.

🚨 This is a phishing email! Here's why:

  • Suspicious sender domain
  • Urgent, threatening language
  • Generic greeting
  • Suspicious links
  • Grammar mistakes
